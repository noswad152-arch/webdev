Reflect on these words

May the God who sees generations at once guide your heart today.

May he lift the weight of fear that says, "everything depends on you"...

...and replace it with the trust that faithfulness is never wasted.

As you plant seeds through words, presence , patience, and prayer,

May you remember that growtth belongs to God.

May you be released from anxiety over outcomes.

May you be rooted instead in obedience, consistency, and love.

Where you have tried to control, may you learn to entrust.

Where you have grown weary, may hope rise again.

So go in peace.

What is done in faith is never forgotten...

And what is is planted in love, will never be lost.
