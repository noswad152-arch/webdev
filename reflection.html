Daily Reflections

Traditionally called a journal. We call it alignment.

Reflections are where you set intention — for the day, the week, or the distant horizon.

You return. You refine. You remember.

The fun part-- is a week from now you can't edit your past reflections. But you can add to them. You'll find that a powerful reminder of how you grow over time.
You can start a new reflection thread or add to an existing one as your story unfolds.

Create a new reflection or append to a previous one. Title + reflection are required.
